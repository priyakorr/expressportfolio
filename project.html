Mission statement

Colossians 1: 28 - we preach, warning every man, and teaching every man in all wisdom; that we may present every man perfect in Christ Jesus.

Vision

  • To share the good news of Gods love and the gospel of Jesus Christ, forgiveness of sin, healing and deliverance, to the people and to nurture them to be Christ-centred, Holy Spirit-filled, doctrinally sound and mature saints in Gods Kingdom.
  • To worship the Lord in spirit and in truth.
  • To provide Systematic Teachings that are theologically sound and balanced.
  • To equip all members to become responsible disciples for full time ministry.
